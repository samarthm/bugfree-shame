UNHRC



Committee large 2 at SMUNC 2015 will be the United Nations Human Rights Council, also known as the UNHRC. The UNHRC is a body created following the establishment of the UN Commission on Human Rights and is an existing subsidiary committee to the United Nations General Assembly. The primary topic to be debated in this committee will be human trafficking.
